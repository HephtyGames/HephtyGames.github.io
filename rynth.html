About the Game

Rynth is an anxiety-inducing experience where you must scour a twisting labyrinth to locate critical medicine and escape a relentless nightmare. This game is not for the faint of heart.

Gameplay

  • Avoid the monsters lurking in the dark—and whatever you do, do not get caught.
  • Three difficulty settings let you dial the tension from eerie to extreme.
  • Atmospheric audio and limited visibility keep you perpetually on edge.

Key Features

  • Procedurally arranged hallways for fresh terror every run.
  • Resource-scarce exploration that forces quick, risky decisions.
  • Simple controls—pure psychological challenge.
