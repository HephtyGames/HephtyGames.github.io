About Me

I am a proud graduate of Drexel University, where I majored in Computer Science with a concentration in Game Development and Human-Computer Interactions. I specialize in programming, level design, and working with Unity and Unreal Engine, with a passion for creating immersive 3D games that anyone can enjoy. My goal is to develop engaging and inclusive experiences that resonate with a wide range of players.
